Hello, welcome to my repository of in-game books of The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim. In my free time I have transcribed books from the video game and have made them available for download to everyone on this website.

"Hasn't this been done yet? Every single book is listed and fully transcribed on the fan wiki."

Well, yes, but no. I have not just written down the contents by hand, but I have also formatted them to look good on individual pages and have converted them into docx then pdf files, so you can print them and read them as real books. I'm also planning on making Kindle versions, which then you can read wherever without the need for a big, bulky bag to hold all that paper. That is, once I get my hands on the tools required to make this possible.
